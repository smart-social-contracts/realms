About These Logos

These organic curve planetary logos represent the most natural approach to the "realms within a finite world" concept. Each design maintains Fibonacci proportional areas while using flowing, asymmetric boundaries that mirror how real geographical regions form through natural processes - rivers, mountains, coastlines, and climate zones.
