Enhanced Geometric Logo Options

Design Enhancement Strategy

Taking your feedback that the original geometric bars were "too plain," I've created enhanced versions that add visual interest while maintaining the clean, institutional aesthetic you want.

Original (Too Plain)

Simple bars, uniform height and color. Clean but lacks visual interest.

Enhanced with Gradients

Subtle gradients add depth, varying heights create rhythm, connecting elements show unity. Professional yet engaging.

Staggered Heights

Different bar heights with subtle shadows create a dynamic, architectural feel. Suggests growth and progress.

Geometric Patterns

Internal patterns (lines, dots, diagonals) add texture and detail. More complex but still institutional.

Recommendation: Enhanced with Gradients


Why this works best:

  • ✅ Subtle gradients add sophisticated depth without being flashy
  • ✅ Varying heights create visual rhythm and movement
  • ✅ Connecting elements suggest unity and collaboration
  • ✅ Still clean and geometric for institutional credibility
  • ✅ Grayscale palette maintains your theme consistency
  • ✅ Scalable design works at any size
