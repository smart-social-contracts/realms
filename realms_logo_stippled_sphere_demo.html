Stippling Art Technique

These logos use the classic stippling technique from traditional art, where dots of varying sizes and densities create the illusion of form, depth, and lighting. The technique naturally represents a 3D sphere through perspective - larger dots appear closer to the viewer, while smaller, more scattered dots recede into the background, creating an authentic planetary appearance.
