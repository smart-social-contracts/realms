Natural Sequence Logo Concepts

Exploring circular logos where division sizes follow natural mathematical sequences rather than equal proportions. These designs reflect how organic systems actually grow and organize - with mathematical harmony found in nature, from nautilus shells to galaxy spirals to plant growth patterns.

Why Natural Sequences?

Equal divisions are artificial and rarely found in nature. Real systems grow according to mathematical principles like the Fibonacci sequence, golden ratio, and prime numbers. These natural proportions create more organic, harmonious, and memorable designs that resonate with our innate sense of beauty and balance.

Fibonacci Sequence

F(n) = F(n-1) + F(n-2)

The most famous natural sequence, found everywhere from nautilus shells to galaxy spirals. Each number is the sum of the two preceding ones.
