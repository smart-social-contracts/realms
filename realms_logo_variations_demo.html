Advantages of Circular Division Logos:

  • Clear conceptual meaning
  • Scalable design system
  • Flexible division counts
  • Professional appearance
  • Memorable and unique
  • Institutional credibility

Considerations:

  • Complex versions may lose detail at small sizes
  • Requires careful color balance
  • May need simplified versions for favicons
  • Border styles affect readability

Final Recommendation

The 4-Division Microsoft-Style logo with white separators provides the optimal balance of recognition, meaning, and usability. It leverages familiar visual patterns while clearly representing the "realms within a finite world" concept. The four-quadrant structure is instantly recognizable, scales perfectly, and provides enough flexibility to represent different realm categories without overwhelming complexity.
