Tennis Ball-Inspired Planetary Logos

Elegant planetary logos inspired by tennis ball curves but modified to avoid direct association. These sophisticated designs use flowing, natural divisions that create beautiful territorial boundaries while maintaining the institutional elegance needed for the Realms platform.

2 Divisions

Dual Hemispheres

Classic two-hemisphere design with elegant flowing curves. Creates a perfect balance between simplicity and sophistication, ideal for minimalist branding.

3 Divisions

Tripartite Flow

Three flowing regions with harmonious curves that create natural territorial boundaries. Perfect balance of complexity and elegance for sophisticated institutional use.

4 Divisions

Quadrant Harmony

Four elegant regions with tennis ball-inspired curves that avoid geometric rigidity. Creates beautiful asymmetric balance while maintaining clear territorial definition.

5 Divisions

Pentagonal Flow

Five sophisticated territories with complex flowing curves. Most detailed design that showcases the full potential of tennis ball-inspired planetary divisions.
